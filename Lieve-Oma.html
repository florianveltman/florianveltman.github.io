an ode to sweet grandmothers
My grandmother is probably the most important person ever to me, as she provided me with the stability and care a child needs growing up.
We all have or have had people helping us become a responsible and caring person, and this short narrative game is an ode to these people.
Grandma forced you to come along a bit, but deep down you know the fresh air is good for you.
She tells you it's the season for penny buns, so look for those! Maybe if you find a lot, Grandma'll treat you to a nice reward?
But in the end you know the mushrooming's just a pretext Grandma came up with, to get you to talk about what's actually going on.
